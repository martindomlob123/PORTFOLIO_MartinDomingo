CAVE studio

Music production collective. I was commissioned to develop an animation for their social media launch, along with a logo that reflected their values and visual identity.

Abuela type

Typography work based on sewing guides from my grandmother's collection. For this project, I had to carry out a restoration process that consisted of scanning and digital cleaning to ultimately obtain a complete vectorized typeface ready for use.

CORREtorre

For this project, I developed the visual identity of a running club, including the design of their jerseys. The chicken symbol emerged from a playful reference within the group: whenever someone couldn't make it to a run, the response was the well-known chicken emoji on WhatsApp.

TODO magazine

This project involved the creation of a niche magazine in A5 format, featuring opinion articles. I developed the full visual identity of the publication, crafted its manifesto and core values, and designed the complete layout.

DnB

Low-cost wine brand from Valencia. Inspired by flyers from the most famous techno clubs, it aims to appeal to young people.

Reciclatumusica.es

This web was made to solve the issue of recycling DVDs and vinyl records in the Madrid region providing a centralized online platform that informs and guides citizens.
